Drawing Board

This website is specifically created to aid students who struggle with visualizing simple geometrical shapes like circles and rectangles. It offers visual representations of these shapes to enhance students' understanding of their properties and characteristics. The goal is to improve students' spatial perception and comprehension of fundamental geometry concepts.



Enjoy scrolling through my website! Cheers!
